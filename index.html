About ACM

ACM TKMCE is the student chapter of the world’s largest computing society — promoting tech, learning, and community.

Vision

ACM's vision is to advance computing as a science and a profession. It seeks to empower students, educators, and professionals with access to the best practices, cutting-edge knowledge, and opportunities in the field of computing.

Mission

ACM's mission is to deliver resources that advance computing as a science and a profession. It provides the computing field's premier Digital Library and serves its members and the computing profession with leading-edge publications, conferences, and career resources.
